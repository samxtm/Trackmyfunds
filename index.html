About Us

Welcome to TrackMyFunds! Our mission is to make personal finance management easier for everyone. Whether you're tracking daily expenses or managing larger budgets, we provide a simple interface to keep you on track.

This web app was created by Sameerraj Cheela with the goal of helping people manage their money more effectively. With TrackMyFunds, you can log your expenses, track your spending habits, and visualize your financial progress.
